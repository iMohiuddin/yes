Message from Chairman

Mr. David Son

YesiT Institute has commenced its travel gloriously in 2020/2018.The teachers of this institution are highly educated and well-trained. Moreover, it is our belief that much more sundry training will widen the periphery of the respected teachers more. With this the modern architecture, rich classroom, computer lab and library of YesiT institute is functioning as helping hand in building students eligible citizen for meeting up the demand of the age. In the age of unrestricted current of information, the knowledge of science and information technology has become inevitable to build more science bent skilled human resource for facing boldly the challenges of twenty first century. I have been pleased very much knowing that YesiT institute cherishing the same goal is being also linked up with the flow of information through opening its own website. My belief and zeal will saturate the students more with the institution and unfold them more and more to use properly the technology of information. I think it is a wise step of inaugurating the website of institution’s own in building a science-pertaining and self-made nation.


I wish its success and hopeful that it will be a useful tool for the students in accomplishing their life and scholastic track. May Allah bless everybody.



Rahat Hossain Pollob

Chairman
